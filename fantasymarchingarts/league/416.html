About Drum Corps Cooperative
Drum Corps Cooperative, a part of Marching Arts Cooperative, is dedicated to providing a league experience for members by members. We are the first and only league to let members vote on leagues activities such as event dates/locations/names, membership acceptance/revocation, elections of the board of directors, etc. We hope you join us in the only league in FMA where you have voice! PM the founder or the board of directors today to get an invite to both the league and league Discord server!
