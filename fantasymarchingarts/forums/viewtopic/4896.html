🎉 Introducing... the FMA Inter-League Battle! 🎉

Get ready for an epic showdown because starting this season, leagues will go head-to-head in the ultimate test of consistency, strategy, and performance!

Here’s how it works:
Every week, I’ll rank each league based on their placements across divisions. The twist? Lower scores are better! Just like golf:

🥇 1st place = 1 point

🥈 2nd place = 2 points
...you get the idea.

I’ll track these weekly totals, and the league with the lowest cumulative score by the Wednesday before Finals takes the crown! 🏆 I'll be keeping score in a master spreadsheet (yes, I love spreadsheets).

🔄 This battle will rotate between circuits, starting with Drum Corps in Season 130, then onto Marching Band, Guard, etc. The winning league each season will nominate a group to host the next Inter-League Smackdown Event, a Masters-of-FMA-style event featuring the best of the best from the previous season with room for others to join in the event that there is a need to fill up the event. Think of it as FAME/CLASS 2.0, but bigger, better, and way more competitive.

💡 A few key details:

Only the top 12 groups per division count each week - so getting as many of your league's members into that top tier matters!

Divisions will be scored separately, then combined into one league total.

Yes, larger leagues may have some advantage, but hey, part of the fun will be working through those quirks together!

This is meant to bring more energy, more rivalry, and more FUN to the game. I’ve seen some newer leagues grow and evolve, and I think this adds a new layer of depth and teamwork.

So—what do you think? 🔥 Yay or Nay?
Questions? Concerns? Suggestions? Bring them on!
(P.S. I’m doing this whether you jump in or not 😎)

Let the battles begin! 💥

Objective Recap:
Each fantasy team has 3 corps

One assigned to Music captions

One to Visual captions

One to GE (General Effect)

Scoring comes from TWO shows per player per week

Use their latest performance of the week Caption Scoring
Music Player Music Ensemble + Brass + Percussion
Visual Player Visual Ensemble + Guard + Visual Proficiency
GE Player GE 1 + GE 2 (or total GE) Selection Rule
Each week, for each corps, use the latest 2 events they performed in - based on specific criteria (there must be at least 3 groups in your division - groups do not have to be in the tournament - corps in your division may be teammates or opponents)

Example:

Week runs Sunday - Saturday

Nemesis could perform Tues + Sat

Spirit of Atlantis performs Mon + Fri (etc,.,) It really depends on your schedule and if you meet the event criteria. Power-Up Rule: “Backup Boost”
✅ Rule Summary
Each fantasy team may designate 1 backup corps at the start of the season. (They are a corps that is not in the tournament and will be chosen from a list)

That backup can be used twice during the tournament, in place of any main player (Music, Visual, or GE) who doesn’t compete in a week.

YOU DO NOT HAVE TO WAIT TO USE IT! If you want to use it to get a higher score, go for it! That is totally up to the team to decide. Example: Team Scoring Breakdown
Team A
Music Corps: Spirit of Atlantis

Music Ensemble – 2nd → 9 pts

Brass – 3rd → 8 pts

Percussion – 5th → 6 pts
Music Total: 23 pts

Visual Corps: Gold Fusion

Visual Proficiency – 4th → 7 pts

Guard – 6th → 5 pts

Visual Ensemble – 4th → 7 pts
Visual Total: 19 pts

GE Corps: Nemesis

GE 1 – 1st → 10 pts

GE 2 – 2nd → 9 pts
GE Total: 19 pts

Team A Weekly Score = 23 + 19 + 19 = 61 points
